About me

Hi there

I am a Spanish stage director based in London. My love for stories began when I started writing my own thoughts about the weekly Ally McBeal episode at the age of seven. Later in life, I started learning about storytelling with Emily St. James's reviews of The Sopranos. I trained at RADA and I am comfortable directing opera, musical theatre, and plays.

I recently directed l'Orfeo for Richmond Opera and Carmen for Opera Integra.

Upcoming engagements:

  • Richmond Twinning Celebration: scenes from l'Orfeo (Director, 8th June 2025, Richmond Opera)

  • Opera Holland Park: Lucia di Lammermoor (Observership, June/July 2025)

  • Southgate Opera: Patience (Director, February 2026)

    • Previous assistant directing credits include: La Bohème (Greenwich Opera), La Vie Parisienne (Southgate Opera), Semele, Venus & Adonis (Richmond Opera), l'Elisir d'Amore (Brent Opera) and La Traviata (Instant Opera).
